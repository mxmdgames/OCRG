Rentals

We offer a variety of watercraft for rent, including inflatable canoes, packrafts, and paddleboards. We also offer life jackets and paddles for rent.

The rental rates for our watercraft are as follows with a guided trip:

  • Inflatable canoe: $25
  • Paddleboard/ Sit on top Kayak: $15

We also offer discounts for large groups and for members of the local paddling club.

Guided Trips

We offer guided trips on the Rough River and the Green River. Our trips are led by experienced guides who will take you to some of the most beautiful spots in the area.

The guided trips we offer are as follows:

  • Rough River: 2-mile trip, $25 per person
  • Rough River: 4-mile trip, $30 per person
  • Rough River: 6-mile trip, $35 per person
