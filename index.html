Rentals

We offer a variety of watercraft for rent, including inflatable canoes, packrafts, and paddleboards. We also offer life jackets and paddles for rent.

The rental rates for our watercraft are as follows:

  • Inflatable canoe: $30 per hour
  • Packraft: $10 per hour
  • Paddleboard: $15 per hour

We also offer discounts for groups of 4 or more and for members of the local paddling club.

Guided Trips

We offer guided trips on the Rough River and the Green River. Our trips are led by experienced guides who will take you to some of the most beautiful spots in the area.

The guided trips we offer are as follows:

  • Rough River: 3-hour trip, $50 per person
  • Green River: 4-hour trip, $60 per person

We also offer discounts for groups of 4 or more.
